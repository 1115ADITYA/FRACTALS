What Are Fractals?

Fractals are infinitely complex patterns that repeat at every scale. Nature uses them in coastlines, snowflakes, and ferns — and now cinema uses them to create impossible worlds.

🎬

CGI & Fractal Algorithms

Visual effects artists apply fractal mathematics to generate realistic terrain, alien landscapes, and mind-bending architecture — all procedurally generated from simple equations.

The Golden Ratio

φ ≈ 1.618. Cinematographers frame shots using this ratio, placing subjects and horizons along golden spirals to create compositions that feel instinctively beautiful to the human eye.
